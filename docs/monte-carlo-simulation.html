Model Assumptions

AssumptionValueRationale
New user acquisitionLinear monthly + growth %NOT compound viral. New users come from ad spend, content marketing, Keith's podcast, social. Monthly acquisition grows slowly as brand awareness increases.
Churn modelMonthly % with varianceRandomized ±40% each month. Premium tiers churn less but blended rate used. Community-based products typically 5-8% monthly.
ARPU calculationBlended across tiersAssumes ~55% Core ($29), ~35% Premium ($79), ~10% Elite ($199). Blended ~$52 base. Randomized ±25% monthly as tier mix shifts.
SeasonalityJanuary surge, summer dipNew Year's resolutions drive Jan/Feb signups (+20-30%). Summer months see -10-15% acquisition. Holiday season mixed.
Launch seed usersKeith's existing clients350+ existing clients; conservative estimate that 100 convert to app at launch from direct outreach.
Cost structureAI API + hosting + supportClaude API ~$0.01/message, ~50 messages/user/month = ~$0.50/user. Hosting ~$200/mo. Primary cost is CAC.

Sources & Footnotes

[1] US married adults: ~133.1M — US Census Bureau, Current Population Survey 2023 ASEC Supplement. 133.1 million married adults age 15+, not counting 4.6M separated. census.gov/marriage-and-divorce

[2] Households earning $250K+: 10.1% — As of 2025, 10.1% of US households (13.7M) earn $250K+. Source: DQYDJ analysis of Census CPS data. dqydj.com/household-income-percentile-calculator. Also supported by: Census Bureau P60-286 (2024 income data)

[3] Marital distress: ~20% — "Approximately 20% of all married couples experience marital distress at any given time." ABCT (Association for Behavioral and Cognitive Therapies) fact sheet. abct.org/fact-sheets/marital-distress. Also: "Up to a third of married individuals report low marital satisfaction" — PMC/Frontiers in Psychology, 2025. PMC11961942

[4] Men seeking help: 17% — "In 2024, around 17% of men received mental health treatment or counseling." Statista, based on SAMHSA NSDUH data. statista.com/673172. Note: Only 7.9% of men specifically attended counseling/therapy sessions (vs 21.2% of women). gatewaycounseling.com

[5] Digital/telehealth adoption: 38% — "Nearly 38% of Americans have utilized telehealth services for medical or mental health needs." Source: APA/telehealth industry surveys 2024. agentsofchangeprep.com. Mental health-specific: "62.3% of telehealth claims had a mental health diagnosis" (Feb 2025). crowncounseling.com

[6] Subscription app churn: ~86% churn in year 1 — "Only 13.8% of monthly subscribers are still active after a year." RevenueCat State of Subscription Apps 2024, analyzing 30,000+ apps. revenuecat.com/state-of-subscription-apps-2024

[7] Customer acquisition cost: $22-175+ — Average Facebook CPL across industries: $21.98 (2024). Coaching/high-ticket services typically 3-8x higher due to audience targeting. flyweel.co/lead-gen-cpl-cac-benchmark. Also: focus-digital.co/facebook-cac-by-industry

[8] Market growth: 11.83% CAGR — Telehealth sector growth rate as of 2024. Online therapy services market projected to reach $17.34B by 2035. GlobeNewsWire/SNS Insider
